Hello There!

Thank you for your interest in my application.
I was excited to receive the challenge and realized that it was a good opportunity to try something new with programming and see what I could make. My experience with programming was with Python and somewhat limited to its uses within GIS, more specifically with projects on ArcGIS. And before my application, I had about one week's worth of knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from working on a Udemy course and after reading the brief I used the first week and a half toget further in the course to try gain enough skills to be able to present my digital solution without depending on ArcGIS or Esri products. so I will admit the challenge has been, well challenging. The code of this project is rough in some parts, kind of like an old dog toy stuffed and stitched with what was available to keep it intact, but I am grateful for the last three weeks and what I have learned.

I would appreciate any feedback on how I could better this project as
I intend to complete it even if my application is unsuccessful.
